<section class="lead">We all have plans. They are bright, beautiful and deeply promising, because humans are optimists and a board deck is no place for doubt. The only flaw is that plans almost never survive contact with the quarter that actually shows up.</section>

So we built a small but powerful app for the morning after. [Forecast App](/tools/forecast/) is here to sober the room up after the team’s triumphant presentation, gently, with [arithmetic](/pressroom/Forecasting-Methods/). It tracks every variable that genuinely moves the number, it updates itself constantly, and it has the rare distinction of taking nobody’s side in a board meeting.

At no extra charge, it also **settles your medium-term strategy**. It asks two questions. Are you defending the bottom of the range and holding the ground you already have, or pushing the top of it higher?

Your answer is the strategy, and more or less the future.

<div class="highlight">Optimism is free. Reality sends an invoice.</div>

Forecast is available now as a per-seat licence, sitting on the systems you already run. Go [see what it does](/tools/forecast/), then open the [live demo](/demo/forecast/cockpit/) and drag the assumptions around until the number does something you don’t like.
