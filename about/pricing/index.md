## Investment in Sanity

We believe that selling software is like selling hammers when what you really need is a house. That’s why we don’t sell software—we sell outcomes, competencies, and fewer sleepless nights for your financial team.

Our pricing philosophy combines two approaches:

**Time & Materials** — Integration and implementation are billed hourly. This covers the setup work: connecting your existing systems, configuring the environment, and making sure everything talks to everything else. Don’t worry, our developers work faster than your accounting department can approve invoices.

**License-Based** — Once everything is up and running, you pay for licenses based on which members of our digital “family” you want working for you. No per-module fees, no surprise add-ons.
